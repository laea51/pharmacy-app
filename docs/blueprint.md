# **App Name**: FarmaciaPlus

## Core Features:

- Patient Account & Order Submission: Patients can register/login, upload medication slips (image/text input), and select pickup from the store or delivery to their address. An AI tool will assist in extracting medication details from the slip.
- Order Tracking & Details: Patients can view their current and past orders, including medication names, quantities, subtotal, and total. Order statuses (Created, Preparing, Ready, Delivered) are clearly displayed.
- Bitcoin Lightning Payment: A unique QR code is generated for each order, allowing customers to pay with their Bitcoin Lightning wallet to confirm the order. Payment confirmation updates order status.
- Clerk Order Management: Pharmacy clerks can view new orders, process them by changing their status (e.g., 'preparing', 'ready'), and manage notification to customers when orders are ready for pickup or delivery.
- Administrator Inventory Management: Admins can add, edit, and remove medication records with details such as name, concentration, presentation (pills, units), quantity, and price. The system also provides low stock alerts.
- Administrator Sales Reports: Admins can access comprehensive daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports, detailing medication quantity sold, unit price, and total sales figures.
- Role-Based Access & Authentication: Secure user authentication system with distinct dashboards and functionalities for Patients, Pharmacy Clerks, and Administrators within the same application.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: Very light cyan-blue (#E8F4F6) for a clean, calm, and trustworthy base.
- Primary color: Muted cyan-blue (#73a7fa) to convey professionalism, trust, and clarity, used for key interactive elements.
- Accent color: Vibrant mint green (#73fa7c) for highlighting important actions, notifications, and success messages.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable qualities, suitable for medical information and detailed reports.
- Use a set of clear, modern, line-style icons relevant to pharmacy operations, delivery, payments, and user roles for intuitive navigation.
- Responsive and intuitive layout ensuring optimal experience across desktops and mobile devices, with ample whitespace for clarity and easy information scanning. The primary language for the UI will be Spanish.
- Subtle and functional animations for user feedback, such as order status updates, payment confirmations, and successful form submissions, enhancing user experience without distraction.
